## Foundations & Standards

- [ISO 20022](https://www.iso20022.org/) – Global standard for electronic data interchange between financial institutions.
- [SWIFT](https://www.swift.com/) – Secure messaging network used by banks and financial institutions worldwide.
- [PCI DSS](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/) – Security standards for handling cardholder data.
- [Open Banking](https://www.openbanking.org.uk/) – Framework enabling secure data sharing between banks and third parties.
- [Financial Data Exchange (FDX)](https://financialdataexchange.org/) – Common API standard for financial data sharing.

## Payments & Money Movement

- [Stripe](https://stripe.com/) – Payment processing platform for online and subscription businesses.
- [Adyen](https://www.adyen.com/) – Global payments platform for enterprises and marketplaces.
- [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/) – Online payments and digital wallet platform.
- [Square](https://squareup.com/) – Payments and point-of-sale tools for businesses.
- [Checkout.com](https://www.checkout.com/) – Cloud-based payment processing platform.
- [Wise](https://wise.com/) – Cross-border payments with transparent exchange rates.
- [Payoneer](https://www.payoneer.com/) – Global payments platform for freelancers and marketplaces.

## Banking & Open Banking

- [Plaid](https://plaid.com/) – API platform connecting apps to users’ bank accounts.
- [Tink](https://tink.com/) – Open banking platform for payments and financial data.
- [TrueLayer](https://truelayer.com/) – Open banking APIs for payments and data access.
- [Yapily](https://www.yapily.com/) – Open banking infrastructure for financial services.
- [Solaris](https://www.solarisgroup.com/) – Banking-as-a-Service platform for embedded finance.

## Cards & Issuing

- [Marqeta](https://www.marqeta.com/) – Card issuing and payment processing platform.
- [Stripe Issuing](https://stripe.com/issuing) – APIs for creating and managing virtual and physical cards.
- [Adyen Issuing](https://www.adyen.com/card-issuing) – End-to-end card issuing solution.
- [Unit](https://www.unit.co/) – Embedded finance platform with card issuing and banking.
- [Synapse](https://synapsefi.com/) – Banking and card issuing APIs for FinTechs.

## Lending & Credit

- [Upstart](https://www.upstart.com/) – AI-driven lending and credit risk platform.
- [Affirm](https://www.affirm.com/) – Buy now, pay later (BNPL) platform.
- [Kabbage](https://www.kabbage.com/) – Small business lending and cash flow tools.
- [Lendio](https://www.lendio.com/) – Online marketplace for small business loans.
- [Zopa](https://www.zopa.com/) – Digital bank offering loans and savings products.

## Wealth Management & Investing

- [Robinhood](https://robinhood.com/) – Commission-free investing platform.
- [Wealthfront](https://www.wealthfront.com/) – Robo-advisor for automated investing.
- [Betterment](https://www.betterment.com/) – Digital wealth management platform.
- [Interactive Brokers](https://www.interactivebrokers.com/) – Trading and brokerage APIs.
- [Alpaca](https://alpaca.markets/) – API-first stock trading and brokerage platform.

## Personal Finance & Budgeting

- [Mint](https://mint.intuit.com/) – Personal finance and budgeting app.
- [YNAB](https://www.ynab.com/) – Budgeting software based on zero-based budgeting.
- [Monzo](https://monzo.com/) – Digital bank with built-in budgeting tools.
- [Revolut](https://www.revolut.com/) – Financial super app with banking, cards, and analytics.
- [Emma](https://emma-app.com/) – Personal finance app for tracking spending and subscriptions.

## Financial Data & Market APIs

- [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/professional/) – Financial data, analytics, and news platform.
- [Refinitiv](https://www.refinitiv.com/) – Market data and financial infrastructure provider.
- [Alpha Vantage](https://www.alphavantage.co/) – Free and paid APIs for market data.
- [Polygon.io](https://polygon.io/) – Real-time and historical financial market data APIs.
- [Quandl (Nasdaq Data Link)](https://data.nasdaq.com/) – Economic and financial datasets.
- [FindBSB](https://findbsb.com.au/api) – Free Australian BSB number lookup API for validating bank branch codes. No authentication required. 18,000+ BSBs updated monthly from AusPayNet.
